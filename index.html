About MeHakkimda

I'm a solo full-stack developer and Computer Engineering student at Marmara University, Istanbul. I design and build complete systems end-to-end -- from database architecture and backend APIs to frontend interfaces and mobile apps.

Istanbul Marmara Universitesi Bilgisayar Muhendisligi ogrencisi ve bagimsiz full-stack gelistiriciyim. Veritabani mimarisinden backend API'lere, frontend arayuzlerden mobil uygulamalara kadar sistemleri bastan sona tasarliyor ve gelistiriyorum.

Every project on this page is self-hosted on my own infrastructure, with CI/CD pipelines, Docker orchestration, and monitoring -- because shipping means owning the full lifecycle.

Bu sayfadaki her proje, CI/CD pipeline'lari, Docker orkestrasyon ve izleme ile kendi altyapimda barindiriliyor -- cunku uretim, tum yasam dongusune sahip olmak demektir.
