FIVUCSAS

// biometric auth platform

Multi-tenant biometric authentication SaaS with N-step MFA, 10 auth methods (face, voice, fingerprint, NFC, TOTP, WebAuthn), OAuth 2.0 / OIDC provider, and an embeddable verification widget. RFC 8176 amr talepleri, JWT tüm adımlar tamamlanana kadar erteleniyor.
